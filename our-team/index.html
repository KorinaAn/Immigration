About Us!

Manvika Gupta

Manvika is currently an MPA/ID student at the Harvard Kennedy School. She has previous experience as an Monitoring and Evaluation Officer with UNICEF, New Delhi, and holds an undergraduate degree in Economics from Shri Ram College of Commerce, New Delhi. She also has experience with a UK-based general insurer as an actuarial consultant in using statistical models to evaluate reserve bookings.

Manuel Ortiz

Manuel Ortiz is an MC/MPA student at the Harvard Kennedy School. He was born and raised in Bogota, Colombia but has been living in California for the past three years. He recently worked at DoorDash in the Bay Area and previously worked for some years as a consultant at McKinsey & Co. Before that he obtained an MBA from Duke University and spent some years as a Military Intelligence officer in the Colombian National Army. Manuel has a BS from the United States Military Academy at West Point.

Steven Harders

Steven is a mid-career MPA student at the Harvard Kennedy School, and an active duty Naval Aviator, flying the MH-60S Knighthawk. He has completed several deployments in support of US security obectives worldwide. He lives in San Diego, California.

Korina Anagnostopoulou

She obtained a BS in Political Science, International Affairs and Economics from the University of Piraeus. She has a background in policy and government work and international organizations, as well as working with NGOs and charitable foundations.
